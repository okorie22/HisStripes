Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create a video?

The video generation process typically takes 3-5 minutes depending on the length and complexity of your content.

What video formats are supported?

We currently support MP4 and WebM formats, which are widely compatible with all social media platforms including YouTube.

Can I edit my video after it's been generated?

Yes, you can go back to previous steps to make changes, then regenerate your video. For more advanced editing, you can download the video and use your preferred editing software.

Are there any limitations on video length?

The free version allows videos up to 3 minutes in length. For longer videos, please contact us about our premium options.
