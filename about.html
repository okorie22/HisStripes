About Us

Professional house call providers serving the DFW metroplex and North Texas regions

Who We Are

HisStripes Health Care Services, PLLC is a dedicated house call provider bringing essential medical care directly to patients throughout the DFW metroplex and North Texas regions. We specialize in providing professional healthcare services in the comfort and convenience of your own home.

Our practice was established to address the critical need for accessible healthcare for individuals who find it difficult or impossible to travel to traditional medical facilities. By bringing our services directly to you, we eliminate transportation barriers and reduce the stress often associated with medical appointments.

Currently accepting new patients, we look forward to serving you with our comprehensive in-home healthcare solutions that prioritize your wellbeing and comfort.
