Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions

HisStripes Health Care Services provides comprehensive house call services, bringing professional healthcare directly to patients throughout the DFW metroplex and North Texas regions. Our services are designed for patient convenience and optimal health outcomes.

Primary Care Services

Our healthcare professionals deliver comprehensive primary care in the comfort of your home:

  • Regular in-home medical visits every 30-45 days
  • Telehealth consultations (audio/video visual)
  • Comprehensive health assessments
  • Chronic disease management
  • Preventive care and wellness guidance

We create personalized care plans tailored to your unique health needs.

Diagnostic & Treatment

We bring essential diagnostic and treatment services directly to your residence:

  • Labs/X-Rays sent directly to patient's home
  • Immunizations (Flu & Pneumonia)
  • E-Scripting of medications
  • Medication deliveries per patient needs
  • DME (Durable Medical Equipment) provided

Our comprehensive approach ensures you receive complete care without leaving home.

Administrative Excellence

We maintain high standards in all aspects of your healthcare management:

  • Timely turnaround time for admission orders, 485s, and other signed documents
  • Flexible scheduling according to each patient's needs
  • PDGM Compliant Documentation
  • Specialist Referrals when needed
  • Insurance coordination and billing assistance

Our efficient processes ensure your care is both excellent and well-documented.
