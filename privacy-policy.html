Privacy Policy

Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal health information

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At HisStripes Health Care Services, PLLC, we are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information in compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and other applicable laws.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide you with the best possible care:

  • Personal identification information (name, date of birth, address, etc.)
  • Contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Insurance and payment information
  • Medical history and health information
  • Information about your healthcare providers
  • Treatment and service records

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal health information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and coordinate your healthcare services
  • To bill and collect payment for services rendered
  • For healthcare operations and administrative purposes
  • To communicate with you about your health and care
  • To improve our services and patient experiences
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal health information to:

  • Healthcare providers directly involved in your care
  • Your health insurance company for billing purposes
  • Business associates who provide services on our behalf (subject to confidentiality agreements)
  • Public health authorities and health oversight agencies as required by law
  • Law enforcement in specific, limited circumstances
  • Others with your written authorization

Your Rights Regarding Your Information

You have the following rights regarding your personal health information:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of your health records
  • The right to request corrections to your information
  • The right to request restrictions on certain uses and disclosures
  • The right to request confidential communications
  • The right to receive an accounting of disclosures of your information
  • The right to be notified in the event of a breach of your unsecured information

Protecting Your Information

We implement appropriate physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your personal health information. Our staff is trained on privacy practices and procedures to ensure the security of your information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and the revised policy will apply to all information we maintain. We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed about how we protect your information.
