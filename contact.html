Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a financial consultation?

You can schedule a financial consultation by filling out our booking form above, calling us directly at (469) 473-9265, or sending us an email at contact@okemokorie.com. Our team will respond promptly to arrange your appointment.

What's the difference between term life and indexed universal life insurance?

Term life insurance provides coverage for a specific period (term) with lower premiums but no cash value accumulation. Indexed Universal Life (IUL) insurance offers lifetime coverage with a cash value component that can grow based on market index performance while providing downside protection. During your consultation, we'll explain the differences in detail and help you determine which option best suits your needs.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for clients who prefer to meet remotely or are located outside our immediate service area. These consultations are conducted via secure video conferencing platforms and provide the same level of personalized attention as in-person meetings.

How long does a typical consultation last?

Initial consultations typically last 60-90 minutes, allowing sufficient time to understand your financial situation, goals, and concerns. Follow-up appointments may be shorter, depending on the complexity of your financial needs and the specific topics being addressed.

What qualifications do your financial professionals have?

All our financial professionals are licensed insurance agents with extensive training in life insurance and financial planning. Our team members hold various industry certifications and regularly participate in continuing education to stay current with financial products, regulations, and planning strategies.
